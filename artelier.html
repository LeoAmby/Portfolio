Leo's Artelier

Build using HTML5 and CSS only, Leo's Artelier is an imaginary art studio that foresees the networking of artists who will have the chance to post their creativity on the platform. It is intended to promote the beautiful creations that are lying hidden in some art warehouse. Furthermore, lovers of art are in a position to find what they need to buy with ease of partnering with existing artists.
